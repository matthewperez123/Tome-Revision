## 1. Design rationale â€” a courtyard, not a grid

The Stoa is the platform's *visual reward destination*. Where Seals and Wisdom are
numbers and medallions, the Stoa is a **place**: a classical courtyard of arch
niches the reader walks through. The design borrows the metaphor literally:

- **One niche per book.** Twelve arches, twelve paintings, 1:1 with the featured
  book experiences (`src/lib/books/registry.ts`). No pagination, no overflow â€”
  the courtyard is complete at twelve.
- **Emptiness is the inventory.** Locked rewards are not greyed-out cards; they
  are *empty niches* â€” a ghost outline of the medallion waiting in the arch,
  with quiet copy naming the book that restores it. Absence is the motivation.
- **Restoration, not unlock.** Passing a Trial *restores* a painting to its
  niche (dust-to-gold materialize), matching `tactileMoments.stoaRestoration`
  in `src/lib/design/motion.ts`. The verb everywhere is *restore*.
- **Shared architectural dialect.** Every painting is drawn in the same
  composition â€” entablature, two fluted columns, semicircular arch, stepped
  plinth, central medallion â€” with only the palette (the book's `laWorlds`
  world palette) and the motif (from the registry entry) varying. The gallery
  therefore reads as one curated building, not twelve unrelated images.

## 3. Provenance rules

- **Every Stoa painting is `Original Tome art`** â€” procedurally generated by
  `scripts/stoa/gen-stoa-art.mjs` from a deterministic per-slug seed. No
  historical source art, no baked-in title/author text anywhere in the SVG.
- The provenance label (`STOA_PROVENANCE_LABEL`) and license note
  (`STOA_LICENSE_NOTE`) are constants in `rewards.ts`; the detail view renders
  both verbatim. Display-and-share-within-Tome only; not public domain, not
  licensed for redistribution.
- **Passages are public domain** â€” short, real excerpts (translation cited for
  Dante, Homer, Marcus Aurelius, Plato). They belong to the book, not to the
  artwork, and are quoted under the passage's own public-domain status.
- Regeneration rule: palette changes in `src/lib/design/tokens.ts`
  (`laWorlds`) must be mirrored into the generator's BOOKS table and re-run â€”
  same convention as `scripts/covers/tome-vector-covers.mjs`.

## 4. Empty / restored state spec

- Name plate: book title + "Awaiting restoration"; border `surface-sunken`.

**RESTORED (unlocked):**

## 7. Accessibility
